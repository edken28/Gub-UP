Key Features

Simple and easy-to-use tools for community health management

Disease Monitoring

Track and monitor disease cases across Gubat with real-time data and comprehensive records for better health management.

Medicine Inventory

Check available medicines in each barangay health center. Know what's in stock before you visit.

Emergency Hotlines

Quick access to emergency contacts. Connect directly with authorities when you need immediate help.

Health Reports

Health workers can submit and manage health reports for accurate record-keeping and analysis.

Citizen Access

Easy-to-use interface for citizens to access health information and community resources.

Secure Authentication

Verified health worker accounts ensure data integrity and secure health information management.
